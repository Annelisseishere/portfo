03 : About me

Who am I

I'm a professional who thrives at the intersection of analytics and creativity. With an educational background that laid the foundation in technical expertise and a career that has honed my skills in real-world scenarios, I've become a trusted voice in interpreting the complex language of data into actionable insights. Colleagues know me as the go-to person for problem-solving and as someone who can always be counted on for thoughtful analysis and a detail-oriented approach.
Outside of work, I'm a lifelong learner, continually exploring new realms of knowledge. I am someone who sees life as a series of puzzles to be solved and stories to be told, with each solution and tale better than the last.

Where I work

My professional path has led me to serve as a Data Analyst at the Slovak transmission system operator for electricity, where I immerse myself in the vital currents of data that flow through the energy sector. In this role, I apply rigorous analytical methods to illuminate patterns and inform decisions that keep the power grid efficient and reliable. My days are dedicated to converting raw numbers into narratives that power progress.
By night, I am a committed Python enthusiast, delving into the language's depths and exploring its vast potential to solve complex problems. Every evening is an opportunity for growth, as I write lines of code that not only refine my skills but also broaden my understanding of how data shapes our world. This balance of professional rigor and personal passion fuels my continuous journey towards mastery in my field.
